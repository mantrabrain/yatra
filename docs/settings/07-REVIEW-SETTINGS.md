# Review Settings Documentation

**Location:** WordPress Admin → Yatra → Settings → Review

---

## ⭐ Review System Overview

The review system allows customers to share their experiences with your trips. Think of this as building social proof and trust through authentic customer feedback. Reviews are powerful marketing tools that help new customers make booking decisions.

---

## 🎛️ Review Configuration

### Enable Reviews
- **Field Type:** Checkbox
- **Default:** Enabled (true) ✅
- **Description:** Allow customers to leave reviews and ratings
- **What it does:** Turns on the entire review system
- **When enabled:**
  - Review forms appear on trip pages
  - Star ratings show in trip listings
  - Customers can submit reviews after trips
  - Reviews display on your website
- **When disabled:**
  - No review forms shown
  - No star ratings displayed
  - Existing reviews are hidden
  - Review system completely turned off
- **Benefits of enabling:**
  - **Social proof:** New customers see real experiences
  - **Trust building:** Reviews build credibility
  - **SEO benefits:** Review content improves search rankings
  - **Feedback loop:** Learn what customers like/dislike
- **Drawbacks of enabling:**
  - Negative reviews possible
  - Review management required
  - Potential fake reviews
- **⭐ Pro Tip:** Always enable reviews - benefits far outweigh the risks

### Require Booking to Review
- **Field Type:** Checkbox
- **Default:** Enabled (true) ✅
- **Description:** Only customers who have booked can leave reviews
- **What it does:** Restricts reviews to verified customers only
- **When enabled:**
  - Only customers with completed bookings can review
  - Reviews are marked as "Verified Purchase"
  - Higher review authenticity
  - Reduces fake reviews
- **When disabled:**
  - Anyone can leave reviews (even non-customers)
  - No verification badge shown
  - Higher risk of fake/spam reviews
- **Benefits of requiring booking:**
  - **Authenticity:** Reviews come from real customers
  - **Quality control:** Better, more detailed reviews
  - **Trust signals:** "Verified Purchase" badges
  - **Spam reduction:** Less fake review activity
- **Benefits of not requiring:**
  - **More reviews:** Higher quantity of reviews
  - **Early reviews:** Get reviews before trips complete
  - **Broader feedback:** Include non-customers who know your service
- **⭐ Pro Tip:** Keep enabled for authenticity - quality reviews are more valuable than quantity

### Auto-Approve Reviews
- **Field Type:** Checkbox
- **Default:** Disabled (false) ❌
- **Description:** Automatically publish reviews without moderation
- **What it does:** Bypasses manual review approval process
- **When enabled:**
  - Reviews appear immediately after submission
  - No admin approval needed
  - Faster review publication
  - Better customer experience
- **When disabled:**
  - Reviews require admin approval before appearing
  - Full moderation control
  - Quality filtering possible
  - Spam protection
- **When to enable:**
  - You trust your customers
  - You want immediate social proof
  - You have low spam risk
  - You want to reduce admin workload
- **When to disable:**
  - You need quality control
  - You're concerned about spam/fake reviews
  - You want to review all content before publication
  - You're in a competitive market with potential sabotage
- **⭐ Pro Tip:** Start disabled, enable once you're comfortable with review quality

### Enable Review Moderation
- **Field Type:** Checkbox
- **Default:** Enabled (true) ✅
- **Description:** Manually approve reviews before they are published
- **What it does:** Requires admin approval for all reviews
- **When enabled:**
  - All reviews go to moderation queue
  - Admin reviews before publication
  - Can edit, approve, or reject reviews
  - Full control over published content
- **When disabled:**
  - Reviews publish immediately (if auto-approve is on)
  - No moderation queue
  - Less admin work required
  - Faster review publication
- **Benefits of moderation:**
  - **Quality control:** Ensure reviews meet your standards
  - **Spam protection:** Filter out spam and inappropriate content
  - **Brand protection:** Remove harmful or fake reviews
  - **Legal compliance:** Ensure reviews meet legal requirements
- **⚠️ Important:** This setting works with auto-approve - if auto-approve is enabled, moderation is bypassed

### Minimum Rating
- **Field Type:** Dropdown Select
- **Default:** 1 Star
- **Description:** Lowest rating value allowed (1-5 stars)
- **What it does:** Sets the minimum star rating customers can give
- **Options:**
  - **1 Star:** Customers can rate 1-5 stars (full range)
  - **2 Stars:** Customers can rate 2-5 stars (no 1-star reviews)
  - **3 Stars:** Customers can rate 3-5 stars (no negative reviews)
  - **4 Stars:** Customers can rate 4-5 stars (mostly positive only)
  - **5 Stars:** Customers can only give 5 stars (perfect scores only)
- **When to use different minimums:**
  - **1 Star:** Standard practice, allows honest feedback
  - **2-3 Stars:** Reduces very negative reviews while allowing criticism
  - **4-5 Stars:** Premium brands that want only positive feedback
  - **5 Stars:** Very restrictive, may reduce review participation
- **Impact on customer behavior:**
  - **Lower minimum:** More honest feedback, but potential for very negative reviews
  - **Higher minimum:** More positive reviews, but less authentic feedback
- **⭐ Pro Tip:** Use 1 star for authentic feedback - negative reviews help you improve

### Review Reminder Days
- **Field Type:** Number Input
- **Default:** 7
- **Description:** Send review reminder email this many days after trip completion
- **What it does:** Automatically sends email asking customers to leave reviews
- **How it works:**
  - System tracks trip completion dates
  - Email sent X days after trip completes
  - Email includes direct link to review form
  - Reminder only sent if customer hasn't already reviewed
- **Common timing strategies:**
  - **3 days:** Fresh experience, but customers might still be traveling home
  - **7 days:** Good balance - customers home but experience still fresh
  - **14 days:** Customers fully settled, but experience less fresh
  - **30 days:** Too late - experience forgotten
- **Best practices:**
  - Don't send too early (customers still traveling)
  - Don't send too late (experience forgotten)
  - Test different timing to see what works for your customers
- **⭐ Pro Tip:** 7 days is optimal for most travel businesses

## 🎯 Best Practices for Review Settings

### 1. Review Collection Strategy
- **Make it easy:** Simple review forms with clear instructions
- **Ask at the right time:** After trip completion, not during
- **Send reminders:** Automated emails increase review rates
- **Incentivize carefully:** Consider rewards for reviews (check local laws)

### 2. Review Moderation
- **Be prompt:** Approve reviews quickly to keep momentum
- **Be fair:** Approve legitimate negative reviews
- **Respond to reviews:** Engage with both positive and negative feedback
- **Learn from feedback:** Use reviews to improve your service

### 3. Review Display
- **Show recent reviews:** Keep content fresh
- **Display ratings prominently:** Star ratings catch attention
- **Show review count:** More reviews = more trust
- **Use review snippets:** Highlight best reviews

### 4. Quality Control
- **Monitor for spam:** Watch for fake or spam reviews
- **Check authenticity:** Ensure reviews come from real customers
- **Maintain standards:** Remove inappropriate or harmful content
- **Be transparent:** Don't delete legitimate negative reviews

## ⚠️ Common Review Issues and Solutions

### 1. No Reviews Coming In
- **Problem:** Customers aren't leaving reviews
- **Causes:** No reminders, difficult process, no incentive
- **Solutions:** Enable review reminders, simplify forms, consider incentives

### 2. Only Positive Reviews
- **Problem:** All reviews are 5 stars, seems fake
- **Causes:** High minimum rating, selective approval
- **Solutions:** Allow full rating range, approve legitimate negative reviews

### 3. Fake or Spam Reviews
- **Problem:** Reviews that aren't from real customers
- **Causes:** No verification, competitors, bots
- **Solutions:** Require booking to review, enable moderation, monitor for patterns

### 4. Very Negative Reviews
- **Problem:** Harsh 1-star reviews that seem unfair
- **Causes:** Unreasonable expectations, competitors, bad experiences
- **Solutions:** Respond professionally, address legitimate issues, moderate if truly inappropriate

## 🚨 Legal and Ethical Considerations

### Review Authenticity
- **Honest feedback:** Don't manipulate or fake reviews
- **Customer consent:** Only publish reviews customers agree to
- **Transparency:** Be clear about how reviews are collected

### Review Moderation
- **Fair process:** Apply consistent standards to all reviews
- **No censorship:** Don't remove legitimate negative feedback
- **Documentation:** Keep records of moderation decisions

### Incentives and Rewards
- **Check local laws:** Some places regulate review incentives
- **Be transparent:** Disclose any incentives or rewards
- **Equal treatment:** Offer same incentives to all customers

### Customer Rights
- **Edit requests:** Allow customers to update their reviews
- **Deletion requests:** Provide option to remove reviews
- **Privacy protection:** Handle customer data appropriately
